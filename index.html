Monefy

Monefy is a lightning-fast expense-tracking app that lets you record any purchase in two taps and see where your money goes instantly.

All data stays on your device or in your own Google Drive / Dropbox; optional passcode & biometric locks add an extra layer of protection.

Servicios

The core app is completely free! If you're looking for a deeper budgeting experience, an optional upgrade unlocks unlimited accounts, recurring transactions, advanced filters and more.

Monefy focuses on simplicity, speed and privacy — you can import statements manually or add expenses in seconds, no bank login required.
